Business Name: SOCIAL LIFT

Ownership

SOCIAL LIFT is a partnership-based business, owned and operated by a group of experienced entrepreneurs from diverse professional backgrounds. Our team consists of individuals with expertise in digital marketing, business administration, communication, technology, and scientific analysis. The founders are committed to building a long-lasting and reputable company that provides high-quality services to small businesses.

Products and Services Offered

  • Social Media Strategy Development: Crafting detailed strategies to help businesses reach their target audience on social media platforms.
  • Content Creation and Management: Creating high-quality, tailored content that engages audiences.
  • Social Media Account Management: Full-service management of social media accounts, ensuring consistent engagement and customer interaction.
  • Social Media Advertising: Running paid campaigns on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter.
  • Analytics and Reporting: Tracking campaign performance to provide actionable insights.
  • Brand Development and Promotion: Helping businesses establish and strengthen their brand presence across social media platforms.
